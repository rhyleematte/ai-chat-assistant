MISSION
“Good food for everyone.”
To serve our community with honest, affordable meals in a warm space where everyone belongs.
VISION
The neighborhood’s table
To become the most loved gathering spot in our community — a place people return to every day.
🤝
Community first
Every decision puts neighbors before profit — we exist to serve the people around us.
💰
Fair pricing always
Quality food should never be a luxury. Affordability is not a compromise, it is our promise.
❤️
Warm hospitality
Every guest feels welcome, seen, and valued from the moment they walk in.
✅
Consistent quality
Affordable never means cutting corners. Every plate is made with care and pride.
1
Build a loyal local customer base
Aim for 200+ repeat customers within the first 6 months through great food and personal service.
2
Keep prices accessible
Maintain a menu where any item can be enjoyed for under ₱200 without sacrificing quality.
3
Create community events & programs
Host weekly specials, barangay partnerships, or student discount programs to deepen local ties.
4
Achieve break-even within year one
Manage food costs below 35% of revenue through smart sourcing and minimal waste.
💵
Revenue streams

🎯
Target customers
Students, workers, families, seniors — everyone in the neighborhood
⭐
Key differentiator
Unbeatable value + a genuinely welcoming atmosphere
📈
Growth path
Word-of-mouth → social media → second branch
